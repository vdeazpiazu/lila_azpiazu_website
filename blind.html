If you are blind...

An smartphone application developed to help blind people, move around the city with public transport. The development was designed after a long research about how blind people use an smartphone and what devices they have to comunicate through ways. The other purpose was to not need government money to improve the comunication. The project was depveloped under SEAT supervision, in a coo- perative university design and development technology devices.
