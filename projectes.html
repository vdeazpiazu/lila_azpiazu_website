NO és NO

Interactive multisensory installations to bring the sexual harassment problem to a concerned society. Through this installations, you can get close to what women feel when they have to hold sexual street haressment. The senses stimulation (hearing, visual and touching) would bring you to the scene, letting you feel feelings like anxiety, nerves, pressure, discomfort, vulnerability... Each piece of the project brings you one of this feelings, and you need all together, to go through the experience.
